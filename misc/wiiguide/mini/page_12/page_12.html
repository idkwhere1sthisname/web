Q&A

Please check the following before contacting Nintendo Customer Support.

  • Please also refer to any documentation for software titles or accessories as appropriate.
Q:

Can WiiConnect24 be used?
A:

No, WiiConnect24 is not supported.
Q:

Can I create, send or receive messages using the Wii Message Board?
A:

You can only use the Wii Message Board to view messages posted to your calendar. You cannot create, send or receive messages.
Q:

Can I use Nintendo GameCube Discs, controllers and accessories?
A:

Discs, controllers and accessories designed for use with Nintendo GameCube or Nintendo GameCube Controller Sockets are not supported.
Q:

The Nunchuk™ or Classic Controller™ (or Classic Controller Pro) won't work with the Wii mini console.
A: First, please check the following.
  • Does the software you are using support the Nunchuk or Classic Controller? You cannot use the Nunchuk or Classic Controller with software which does not feature the appropriate corresponding icon on the packaging.
  • Is the plug correctly inserted? The Nunchuk or Classic Controller plug should be inserted into the Wii Remote until you hear a click.
If the Wii Remote accessory still does not work properly after checking the above, then it is possible that the accessory’s neutral position is inaccurate. To reset to the correct neutral position, make sure you are not touching any buttons or Control Sticks, then press and hold , , and on the Wii Remote simultaneously for three seconds.
  • The term "neutral position" refers to when the buttons or Control Sticks of a Wii Remote accessory are not pressed or tilted. If buttons are pressed or a Control Stick is tilted when powering on the console or connecting Wii Remotes, the neutral position will be inaccurate, causing the Wii Remote accessory to not function normally.
Q:

How do I free up space in the Wii System Memory?
A:

You can erase any unnecessary data in Wii Options.
Q:

Is it possible to exchange data with Nintendo DS systems?
A:

No, the Wii mini console is not capable of communication with any systems in the Nintendo DS family, including Nintendo 3DS.
