A keyboard will appear when you need to enter text. There are two kinds of keyboard: the QWERTY keyboard and the mobile-phone-style keyboard.

Mobile Phone Keyboard

Select character entry type

Select character to enter. Point at a key and press to cycle through the characters on that key in order.

For example, the characters on the “abc” key appear in the order: a→b→c, so pressing twice will give the letter “b”. (Press to cycle through characters in reverse order.)
