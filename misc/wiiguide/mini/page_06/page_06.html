Data Management

You can use Wii Options to erase data saved to the Wii System Memory. Follow the steps below.

  • Manage data using Wii Options if there is not enough free space to save data used by the Wii Menu.

1 Select (Wii Options).

2 Select DATA MANAGEMENT.

3 Select the type of data.

4 Select the data.

5 Select ERASE.

  • The size of the data in blocks will be listed in the area marked in the screenshot to the right with .

Data Management Precautions

  • Deleted data cannot be recovered.
  • Data on the Wii mini console cannot be moved or copied.

Important Privacy Information

You will be able to participate in a variety of games and activities through your Wii mini console. In order to protect your privacy and that of your family when using your Wii mini console, we strongly recommend the following:

  • You should supervise your children's use of the Wii mini console at all times and assist them with the setup of the Wii mini console.
  • Please be sure to clean/format the console before you sell or donate/transfer it. Information about how to wipe your Wii mini console clean of all such information can be found under "Format Wii System Memory" (→).
  • Do not use any personal information on the Wii mini console, such as using your name as a nickname for Mii characters you create.
