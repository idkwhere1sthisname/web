Mii Channel

The Mii Channel lets you choose combinations of facial components and hairstyles to create your own Mii™ characters.

  • Mii characters used in Wii software must be created using this channel. You cannot use Mii characters created using the Wii U Menu.

Creating Your Mii

Create your Mii by choosing the facial components shown on the screen.

Favorites

Mii characters for which the Favorite option () is selected are given priority for appearing in software that is compatible with Mii characters. Up to 10 Mii characters can be marked as favorites.

 

Mingle

The Mingle feature is not used on the Wii U console.

Navigating the Mii Plaza
