About Temporary Pairing

As this method of pairing is only temporary, performing any of the following actions will cause the connection to be lost, requiring you to reconnect the Wii Remote.

  • Starting or closing a software title
  • Returning to the Wii Menu
  • Selecting RESET from the HOME Menu
