Using Keyboards

The keyboard screen will appear in certain programs when you need to enter text. You can choose between two keyboard layouts: QWERTY style and cell-phone style.

QWERTY Keyboard

Shows characters that have been entered.
Select arrows to scroll up or down.

Shows predicted words. Select arrows to scroll through available words.

Switch between uppercase and lowercase characters. (You can also switch to uppercase by holding on the Wii Remote.)

Change the language of the predicted-
word feature.

Change keyboard styles.
Turn predicted-word feature on or off.
Delete character.
Insert carriage return.
Change keyboard to additional letters
or symbols.

Cell-Phone Keyboard

Change character entry between uppercase and lowercase characters or numerals.

Use or to select which character to use from each key.
