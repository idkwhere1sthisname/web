Transferring Data

If you own a Wii console, you can transfer all its software and save data to your Wii U™ console at once.

  • Individual pieces of data cannot be transferred individually.
  • A Nintendo Network ID is required to transfer your data. For more information, see the "User Settings" section of the Wii U Electronic Manual ( ).

You Will Need:

In addition to your Wii U system, you will need the following in order to transfer your data.
  • Wii console (must be set up and operational)
  • SD or SDHC Memory Card (sold separately; 512 MB of free space required)
  • Broadband Internet connection
For more details about Internet-connection settings, see the manuals for the Wii U and Wii consoles.

Transferring Your Data

The transfer process is done using the Wii Remote to operate the Wii U and Wii consoles in turn. Select the Wii System Transfer icon from the Wii Menu on your Wii U console, and follow the on-screen instructions.
  • On-screen instructions are available in English, Spanish, and French. For detailed instructions in Portuguese, please visit support.nintendo.com/wiiu/transferdata.

Precautions about Transferring Data

  • The data will be deleted from the Wii console after it is transferred.
  • Data cannot be transferred from a Wii U console to a Wii console.
  • Once the data has been transferred to a Wii U console, it cannot be moved back to the Wii console.
  • Software that was preinstalled on your Wii console at the time of purchase or is no longer available for download through the Wii Shop Channel will not be transferred.
  • Data cannot be transferred if the country you've selected in System Settings (in the Wii U Menu) doesn't match the country set for your Nintendo Network ID.
  • The Wii Menu on the Wii U console does not support WiiConnect24™. Features in Wii software that use WiiConnect24 will no longer work on the Wii U console.
  • Certain accessories supported by software cannot be used with the Wii U console. For more information, see the "Wii Accessories" section of the Wii U Electronic Manual ( ).
